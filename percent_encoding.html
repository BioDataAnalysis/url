    Percent Encoding¶

    Description¶

    The functions below allow percent encoding and decoding, most commonly used in URL paths, query parameter and fragments.

    Headers¶

    #include <skyr/percent_encoding/percent_encode.hpp>
#include <skyr/percent_encoding/percent_decode.hpp>
